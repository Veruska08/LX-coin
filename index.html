IxCoin is a decentralized, open-source digital currency that operates over a peer-to-peer network and is based on Bitcoin. It was created in May 2011, shortly after Namecoin (DNS). IxCoin uses IxCoins as its unit of currency, similar to how Bitcoin uses BTC, and is represented as "IXC." However, unlike physical currency, IxCoin is intangible, as it is simply a number associated with an IxCoin Address, which is a string of 33 numbers and letters starting with "x." IxCoin transactions offer low costs, irreversibility, anonymity, and decentralization
